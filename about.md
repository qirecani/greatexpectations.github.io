This is a blog about interesting mathematics written informally.

The goal is not to present polished textbook treatments, but to work through good problems, clean ideas, and useful heuristics in a way that is readable and honest.

I expect most posts to be short notes on probability, analysis, geometry, combinatorics, and any other topic that seems worth understanding properly.

Some posts will be complete solutions. Others will just be partial explorations, computations, or derivations that are too nice to lose.
